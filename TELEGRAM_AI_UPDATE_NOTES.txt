TELEGRAM AI UPDATE NOTES

New behavior:
1. Telegram first tries local command/knowledge.
2. If local bot has no answer, AI fallback answers using current bot data only.
3. AI must not invent levels; prompt contains exact bot data.
4. The answer is cached in data/telegram_ai_cache.json.
5. Same question + same market snapshot returns cached answer later.

New commands:
/market_context - shows intraday bias, M15/H1/H4 bias, supply, demand, EQ, liquidity.
/bias - alias of /market_context.
/intraday_bias - alias of /market_context.
/supply_demand - shows supply and demand level only.
/sd - alias of /supply_demand.
/ask [question] - asks AI using bot data snapshot.

Example:
/ask supply demand sekarang dimana?
/ask bias intraday sekarang apa?
